                              AI MODELS USED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🤖 Whisper AI (OpenAI)
   • Purpose: Speech-to-text transcription
   • Model: Base (faster) or Large (more accurate)
   • Languages: 90+ supported

👁️ OpenCV Computer Vision
   • Purpose: Visual analysis
   • Features: Motion, faces, edges
   • Real-time processing

🎬 MoviePy + FFmpeg
   • Purpose: Video editing
   • Features: Cutting, concatenating, encoding
   • GPU acceleration support

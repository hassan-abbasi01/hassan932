# Video Summarization - Quick Start Guide

## What is Video Summarization?

Video Summarization automatically creates a condensed version of your video by:
- 🎬 Detecting important visual moments
- 💬 Identifying key dialogue
- ✂️ Cutting out less important parts
- 🎯 Creating a highlight reel

**Result:** A shorter video that captures the essence of your content!

---

## How to Use (3 Simple Steps)

### Step 1: Upload Your Video
1. Go to the Features page
2. Click "Upload Video" or drag & drop
3. Wait for upload to complete

### Step 2: Configure Summarization
1. Click the "Summarization" tab
2. Choose **Summary Length**:
   - **Short (15%)**: Quick highlights only
   - **Medium (30%)**: Balanced summary ⭐ *Recommended*
   - **Long (50%)**: Comprehensive coverage

3. Choose **Focus**:
   - **Balanced**: Mix of visuals and audio ⭐ *Recommended*
   - **Action & Motion**: Sports, gaming, fast scenes
   - **Speech & Dialogue**: Interviews, talks, conversations

### Step 3: Generate Summary
1. Click "Summarize Video"
2. Wait for processing (progress bar shows status)
3. Download your summarized video!

## Tips for Best Results

### ✅ Do's
- ✅ Use videos at least 30 seconds long
- ✅ Ensure good audio quality for speech-focused summaries
- ✅ Use stable lighting and clear visuals
- ✅ Choose focus that matches your content type
- ✅ Wait for processing to complete

### ❌ Don'ts
- ❌ Don't use extremely short videos (<10 seconds)
- ❌ Don't interrupt processing midway
- ❌ Don't expect speech detection in silent videos
- ❌ Don't use corrupted or damaged video files

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How does it decide what's important?
**A:** The AI analyzes:
- Visual motion and activity
- Presence of people (faces)
- Speech content and keywords
- Scene changes and transitions
- Visual complexity

### Q: Can I manually select segments?
**A:** Currently automatic only. Manual selection coming in future update!

### Q: What happens to the audio?
**A:** Audio is preserved with the selected video segments.

### Q: Does it work with all languages?
**A:** Visual analysis works with all videos. Speech detection supports 90+ languages including English, Urdu, Arabic, Spanish, French, German, and more.

### Q: Is my original video deleted?
**A:** No! Your original video is kept safe. The summary is a separate file.

### Q: Can I create multiple summaries?
**A:** Yes! You can create different summaries with different settings from the same video.

### Q: What if I don't like the result?
**A:** Try different settings:
- Change the length (shorter/longer)
- Change the focus (action/speech/balanced)
- Generate a new summary

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Processing takes too long
**Solutions:**
- Use a shorter video
- Choose "Short" length
- Ensure no other heavy tasks running
- Check internet connection if using cloud

### Problem: No important moments detected
**Solutions:**
- Use "Long" summary length
- Try "Balanced" focus
- Ensure video has varied content
- Check video is not corrupted

### Problem: Missing important parts
**Solutions:**
- Use "Long" length instead of "Short"
- If dialogue missing, use "Speech" focus
- If action missing, use "Action" focus

### Problem: Error during processing
**Solutions:**
- Verify video file is not corrupted
- Check video format (MP4, AVI, MOV supported)
- Ensure enough disk space
- Try re-uploading the video
